SIGNAL-05 is satisfied because both EV/EBIT and earnings yield appear as row fields and earnings_yield is the folded scoring input.

Purpose: turns the new raw factors into rank-affecting score, equally weighting each dimension of cheapness, while preserving the Phase-5 average-over-present and negative-routing contracts (D-01 / D-01b) and 100% backward compatibility with the 45 existing offline tests.

Output: extended `stock_screener.py` (config block + `overall_score` + `process_ticker` + `write_json`) and a new offline `tests/test_scoring_phase6.py`.
